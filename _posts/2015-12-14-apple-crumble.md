### Servings
12

### Ingredients
- Filling:
-   3 cups apples
-   1 cup sugar
-   1/8 cup cornstarch
-   1 tsp cinammon

- Crumble:
-   1 cup flour
-   1/2 cup sugar
-   1/2 cup oleo
-   1/4 cup oatmeal

### Directions
1. Heat oven to 350
2. Mix filling and place into an 8x8 pan
3. Bake 45 minutes - 1 hour
4. Place crumble on top and bake another 15 minutes
5. Variations:
6. Can also make as a rhubarb apple crumble
