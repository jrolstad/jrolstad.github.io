### Servings
12

### Ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 1/2 cups frozen mixed berries

### Directions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
2. Whisk together dry ingredients
3. Cut in butter
4. Mix in buttermilk
5. Mix in berriers
6. Form into two separate circles on parchment
7. Cut each circle into 6 wedges
8. Bake for 20 minutes
