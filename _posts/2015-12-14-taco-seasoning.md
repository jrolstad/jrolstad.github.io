### Servings
6

### Ingredients
- 1 Tbsp chili powder
- 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp. onion powder
- 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
- 1/4 tsp oregano
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1 1/2 tsp. cumin
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1/4 tsp corn starch 


### Directions
1. Combine all above ingredients.
2. Add above mix with 1/2 cup water and
3. 2-3 cups of diced/shredded chicken/pork.
4. Heat and serve
